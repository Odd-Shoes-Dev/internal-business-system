## 🎯 PRICING STRATEGY TIPS

### 1. Start Higher Than You Think
- Easier to discount than raise prices
- You provide real value
- Professional businesses can afford it

### 2. Anchor to Value, Not Cost
- "Save 40 hours/month on manual accounting" = $500+ value
- "Never miss a booking or double-book" = $$$
- "Know your real profitability" = priceless

### 3. Offer Annual Discount
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces churn
- Predictable revenue

### 4. Don't Compete on Price
- There will always be cheaper options
- Compete on value, reliability, support
- Premium pricing = premium perception

### 5. Test & Iterate
- A/B test pricing pages
- Survey customers on willingness to pay
- Adjust based on conversion rates

## 📋 BILLING WORKFLOW

### Month 0: Sign Up
1. Company registers (free trial starts)
2. 30-day trial begins
3. Email welcome + setup guide
4. Onboarding call (optional, for Pro+)

### Days 7, 14, 21: Trial Emails
- Day 7: "Getting value? Here's advanced features"
- Day 14: "50% through trial - need help?"
- Day 21: "7 days left - secure launch discount"

### Day 28: Payment Due
- Email: "Trial ending - choose plan"
- Payment link (Flutterwave/Stripe)
- Option to extend trial if needed (grace period)

### Day 31-35: Grace Period
- System still works
- Banner: "Payment overdue"
- Daily email reminders

### Day 36: Soft Block
- Read-only mode
- Can view data, can't add new
- Prominent payment banner

### Day 45: Hard Block
- No access
- Data preserved for 30 days
- Final notice

### Successful Payment
- Immediate reactivation
- Thank you email
- Invoice sent

## 🚨 HANDLING PAYMENT ISSUES

### Common Scenarios:

**1. "Too expensive"**
- Response: Offer Starter plan or module-only pricing
- Show ROI calculation
- Offer payment plan (quarterly)

**2. "Can we try longer?"**
- Response: Extend 14 days max (once)
- Require payment method on file

**3. "We're seasonal business"**
- Response: Pause subscription option
- Reduced rate during off-season
- Pay annually for discount

**4. "Currency issues"**
- Response: Accept local currency
- Bank transfer option
- Agent/reseller payment
